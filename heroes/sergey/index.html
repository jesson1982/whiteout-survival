Sergey Build Guide - Best All-Around Hero

Last Updated: June 2026 | Tier: S | Role: DPS

Hero Overview

Sergey is the most versatile hero in Whiteout Survival, offering excellent damage output while maintaining good survivability. He's perfect for both PvE and PvP content.

Skill Build Priority

  1. Active Skill: Max first - primary damage source
  2. Passive Attack: Second priority
  3. Passive Defense: Third for survivability

Best Gear

  • Weapon: Legendary Sword (Attack +30%)
  • Armor: Steel Plate (Defense +25%)
  • Accessory: War Banner (HP +20%)
