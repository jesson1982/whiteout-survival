Mason Build Guide - Crowd Control Expert

Last Updated: June 2026 | Tier: A | Role: Support

Hero Overview

Mason excels at controlling the battlefield with stuns, slows, and debuffs. He's essential for alliance wars where coordination is key.

Skill Build Priority

  1. Stun Ability: Max first - disables enemies
  2. Slow Aura: Second for area control
  3. Buff Skill: Third for team support

Best Gear

  • Weapon: Control Staff (CC Duration +30%)
  • Armor: Mystic Robe (Cooldown Reduction +25%)
  • Accessory: Wisdom Orb (Effect Hit Rate +20%)

Team Synergies

Pair Mason with burst DPS heroes like Jesse. His CC sets up perfect kill opportunities.
