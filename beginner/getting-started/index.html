How to Start Whiteout Survival - First 7 Days Guide

Last Updated: June 2026 | Reading Time: 15 minutes

Day 1-3: Foundation Building

Your first three days are crucial for establishing a strong foundation. Focus on these priorities:

  • Upgrade Furnace to Level 5: This unlocks essential buildings and features
  • Build Resource Buildings: Prioritize Wood and Food production
  • Complete Tutorial Quests: Earn valuable rewards and learn mechanics
  • Join an Alliance Early: Access alliance benefits and protection

Day 4-7: Expansion & Optimization

Now that you have the basics, it's time to expand and optimize:

  • Unlock Hero System: Start collecting and upgrading heroes
  • Train Your First Troops: Build military capacity gradually
  • Explore the Map: Find resource nodes and clear barbarian camps
  • Participate in Events: Earn extra rewards from time-limited activities
