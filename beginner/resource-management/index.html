Whiteout Survival Resource Management Guide

Last Updated: June 2026 | Reading Time: 10 minutes

Four Core Resources

🪵 Wood

Used for building construction and upgrades. Collect from forests and lumber mills.

🍖 Food

Essential for troop training and maintenance. Farm from farms and hunting grounds.

⚫ Coal

Required for advanced buildings and research. Mine from coal deposits.

🔩 Iron

Critical for military buildings and equipment. Extract from iron mines.

Collection Optimization Tips

  • Upgrade Resource Buildings: Higher level = more production
  • Use Gathering Teams: Send heroes to collect from map nodes
  • Join Active Alliance: Access alliance resource tiles
  • Complete Daily Quests: Earn bonus resources regularly
