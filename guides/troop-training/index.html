Scouting Strategy:

  1. Send Scouts: Always scout enemy bases before attacking
  2. Analyze Troop Composition: Check which troop type dominates their army
  3. Counter Accordingly: Train troops that counter their main force
  4. Adjust Heroes: Use heroes that boost your counter troop type

Troop Training Priority by Tier

As you progress, you'll unlock higher tier troops. Here's when to transition between tiers:

Tier Furnace Level Priority Notes
T1 Lv.1-5 Transitional Only train enough for early quests
T2 Lv.6-10 Short-term Upgrade to T3 as soon as possible
T3 Lv.11-20 Mid-game Core Your main army for most of mid-game
T4 Lv.21-25 High Priority Significant power jump, prioritize unlocking
T5 Lv.26+ Endgame Goal Maximum tier, requires heavy investment

💡 Pro Tip:

Don't rush to train higher tier troops if you can't afford them! It's better to have a full T3 army than a half-empty T4 army.

Training Speed-Up Tips

Optimize your troop training with these efficiency strategies:

📊 Queue Management

  • Always keep training queues full
  • Train during speed-up events
  • Use training speed-ups strategically
  • Batch train during offline periods

🏛�?Building Upgrades

  • Upgrade Barracks for faster training
  • Research military tech bonuses
  • Unlock additional training queues
  • Max out training speed research

👥 Alliance Benefits

  • Join active alliance for buffs
  • Use alliance help for speed boosts
  • Participate in alliance events for rewards
  • Request reinforcements when needed

💎 Resource Management

  • Stockpile food before mass training
  • Trade excess resources for food
  • Complete daily quests for training items
  • Save speed-ups for critical moments
