Research Basics & Mechanics

The Research Institute is one of your most important buildings. It offers two separate technology trees that complement each other.

How Research Works:

📚 Prerequisites

  • Research Institute must be at required level
  • Previous tier technology must be completed
  • Sufficient resources (Wood, Food, Coal, Iron)
  • Available research queue

⏱️ Research Time

  • Varies by technology tier (5min - 7 days)
  • Can be reduced with speed-up items
  • Alliance help reduces time by 1% per help (max 25%)
  • Hero skills can boost research speed

💰 Resource Cost

  • Higher tiers cost exponentially more
  • Different tech requires different resource ratios
  • Plan resource allocation carefully
  • Trade excess resources before major research

💡 Pro Tip:

Always start long research projects before going offline or sleeping. Use alliance help requests immediately to maximize time savings!

Military Technology Priority

Military tech directly impacts your combat effectiveness. Here's the optimal research order:

🎯 Tier 1 Military Tech (Essential)

Priority 1

Troop Capacity I → II → III

Effect: +10%/15%/20% Army Size

Why First: More troops = more power. This is the foundation of military strength.

Priority 2

Infantry/Ranged/Cavalry Attack I

Effect: +10% Damage for specific troop type

Why Second: Choose based on your main army composition. Maximize your strongest troop type first.

Priority 3

Troop Health I

Effect: +10% All Troop HP

Why Third: Survivability matters. Keeps your troops alive longer in battles.

⚔️ Tier 2 Military Tech (Important)

Priority 4

March Speed I → II

Effect: +10%/15% Movement Speed

Importance: Critical for events, rallies, and quick responses to attacks.

Priority 5

Attack Bonus II → III

Effect: +15%/20% Damage (upgrade from Tier I)

Importance: Continue boosting your primary troop type's damage output.

Priority 6

Defense Bonus I → II

Effect: +10%/15% Damage Reduction

Importance: Reduces troop losses in battles. Important for sustained warfare.

🏆 Tier 3 Military Tech (Advanced)

Priority 7

Load Capacity I → II

Effect: +15%/25% Resource Carrying Capacity

Use Case: Essential for resource raids and event farming.

Priority 8

Healing Speed I → II

Effect: +15%/25% Faster Troop Recovery

Use Case: Reduces downtime after battles. Crucial for active PvP players.

Priority 9

Specialized Troop Tech

Effect: Advanced bonuses for specific scenarios

Use Case: Situational benefits. Research based on your playstyle.

Economy Technology Priority

Economy tech fuels your entire progression. Never neglect this branch!

💎 Tier 1 Economy Tech (Foundation)

Priority 1

Gathering Speed I → II → III

Effect: +15%/25%/35% Resource Collection Rate

Critical: The most important economy tech. Impacts all resource acquisition.

Priority 2

Production Boost I → II

Effect: +10%/15% Passive Resource Generation

Critical: Free resources while offline. Compound benefit over time.

Priority 3

Storage Capacity I → II

Effect: +20%/30% Resource Warehouse Space

Important: Prevents resource waste. Must keep pace with production.

📈 Tier 2 Economy Tech (Growth)

Priority 4

Construction Speed I → II

Effect: +10%/15% Faster Building Upgrades

Value: Accelerates overall progression. Time saved compounds significantly.

Priority 5

Research Speed I → II

Effect: +10%/15% Faster Technology Research

Value: Meta-benefit. Makes all future research faster.

Priority 6

Training Speed I → II

Effect: +10%/15% Faster Troop Training

Value: Reduces army rebuilding time. Important for active players.

🌟 Tier 3 Economy Tech (Optimization)

Priority 7

Resource Protection I → II

Effect: +15%/25% Resources Protected from Attacks

Situational: Valuable if you're frequently targeted in PvP.

Priority 8

Trade Efficiency I → II

Effect: Better exchange rates in Trading Post

Situational: Useful for resource management and conversion.

Priority 9

Expedition Bonuses

Effect: Improved rewards from expeditions

Situational: Good for AFK resource generation.

Early Game Research Path (Furnace Lv.1-15)

In early game, focus on establishing a strong economic foundation while maintaining basic military capability.

Phase 1: Foundation (Lv.1-5)

  1. Gathering Speed I
  2. Troop Capacity I
  3. Production Boost I
  4. Main Troop Type Attack I

Phase 2: Expansion (Lv.6-10)

  1. Gathering Speed II
  2. Storage Capacity I
  3. Troop Health I
  4. March Speed I

Phase 3: Development (Lv.11-15)

  1. Production Boost II
  2. Troop Capacity II
  3. Construction Speed I
  4. Attack Bonus II (main troop type)

Mid Game Research Strategy (Furnace Lv.16-25)

Mid game requires balancing military expansion with economic sustainability.

Recommended Approach:

Week 1-2: Complete all Tier 1 economy tech

Week 3-4: Focus on Tier 2 military tech

Week 5-6: Alternate between military and economy Tier 2

Ongoing: Keep Research Institute upgrading

⚠️ Common Mistake:

Don't rush into Tier 3 military tech before completing essential economy upgrades. You'll run out of resources to sustain your army!

Late Game Optimization (Furnace Lv.26+)

In late game, every research point matters. Specialize based on your role in the alliance.

PvP Specialist Path:

  • Max out all military attack bonuses
  • Prioritize March Speed III
  • Research Healing Speed II
  • Complete Defense bonuses

Economy/Support Path:

  • Max all economy tech first
  • Focus on Gathering Speed III
  • Research Load Capacity II
  • Help alliance members with resources

Balanced Path (Recommended):

  • Alternate between military and economy
  • Keep both branches relatively even
  • Adapt based on current needs
  • Prepare for both PvE and PvP content

Research Speed-Up Tips & Tricks

Maximize your research efficiency with these strategies:

👥 Alliance Help

  • Request help immediately after starting research
  • Help alliance members daily (reciprocal benefit)
  • Join active alliance for maximum help availability
  • Each help = 1% time reduction (max 25%)

🦸 Hero Skills

  • Use heroes with research speed bonuses
  • Activate skill items before long research
  • Some heroes provide passive research boosts
  • Check hero details for research-related abilities

🎁 Event Timing

  • Save speed-up items for research events
  • Participate in "Fastest Researcher" competitions
  • Event buffs often include research speed
  • Plan major research around event schedules

📊 Queue Management

  • Always have research running (never idle)
  • Start longest research before offline periods
  • Use short research for active play sessions
  • Batch similar-tier research together

Research Resource Planning:

Tier 2 500K-2M 2hr-12hr Stockpile resources

Conclusion

Research priority in Whiteout Survival should balance immediate military needs with long-term economic growth. Start with Gathering Speed and Troop Capacity, then expand based on your playstyle and alliance role.

Remember: research is a marathon, not a sprint. Consistent daily progress will outperform sporadic bursts. Always keep your Research Institute upgrading, utilize alliance help, and plan your research schedule around events and offline periods!
