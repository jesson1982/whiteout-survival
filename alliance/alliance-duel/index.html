How to Win Alliance Duels

Last Updated: June 2026 | Reading Time: 10 minutes

Preparation Phase

  • Scout Enemy: Use scouts to identify weak targets
  • Coordinate Timing: Attack simultaneously with alliance
  • Prepare Troops: Max out military buildings before duel

Battle Tactics

  1. Target enemy's resource buildings first
  2. Use heroes with high siege damage
  3. Reinforce allies under attack immediately
  4. Save reserves for counter-attacks

Hero Selection for Duels

Best Heroes: Jesse (siege damage) + Bruce (defense) + Mason (CC)
