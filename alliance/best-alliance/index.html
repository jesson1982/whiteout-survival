How to Find the Best Alliance

Last Updated: June 2026 | Reading Time: 8 minutes

What Makes a Top Alliance?

  • Active Members: At least 80% daily active players
  • Strong Leadership: Experienced R4/R5 officers
  • Regular Events: Participates in all alliance activities
  • Good Communication: Active Discord/Telegram group

How to Apply

  1. Check alliance requirements (Furnace level, power)
  2. Send application with your stats
  3. Join their Discord for interview
  4. Prove activity for 3-5 days

Benefits of Top Alliances

🎁 Daily Rewards

Extra resources and speed-ups

🛡️ Protection

Alliance shield and reinforcement

⚔️ War Support

Coordinated attacks and defense
